About us

Company vision

Sanjeevi's vision for the next phase of development is to 'Touch a Billion Lives'.

Mission Statement

“Our mission is to bring healthcare of International standards within the reach of every individual. We are committed to the achievement and maintenance of excellence in education, research and healthcare for the benefit of humanity”.


Sanjeevni Hospitals and Healthcare was established in 2005 by Dr.Sanjeevni, renowned as the architect of modern healthcare in India. As the nation's most affordable corporate hospital and health centre, Sanjeevi Hospitals and Healthcare is acclaimed for pioneering the private healthcare revolution in the country.


The cornerstones of Sanjeevni's legacy are its unstinting focus on clinical excellence, affordable costs, modern technology and forward-looking research & academics. Sanjeevni Hospitals was among the first few hospitals in the world to leverage technology to facilitate seamless healthcare delivery. The organization embraced the rapid advancement in medical equipments across the world, and pioneered the introduction of several cutting edge innovations in India.


Sanjeevi Hospitals has championed numerous social initiatives to cite a few which assist underprivileged children - SACHi (Save a Child's Heart Initiative) which screens and provides paediatric cardiac care for congenital heart diseases, SAHI (Society to Aid the Hearing Impaired) and the CURE Foundation focused on cancer care.
